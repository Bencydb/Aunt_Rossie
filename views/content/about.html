Aunt Rosie’s Homemade Pies and Preserves

Welcome to Aunt Rosie’s Homemade Pies and Preserves! Since the 1980s, Aunt Rosie has been making delicious, homemade meat pies and preserves using fresh, locally sourced ingredients. What started at farmers markets and home shows has grown into a beloved brand, known for quality and flavor.

Our Products

We offer a variety of meat pies, including:

  1. Steak and Stout Pie
  2. Steak and Kidney Pie
  3. Rosemary Chicken Pie
  4. Turkey Leek Pie

Our seasonal preserves include:

  1. Dill Pickles
  2. Sweet Peaches
  3. Sugar Beets
  4. Hot Pepper Mix
  5. Homemade Hot Horseradish

Pies are available in single-serving and family-size portions, while preserves come in different jar sizes.

Our Commitment

We use only fresh, local ingredients with no preservatives, ensuring great taste and quality in every bite.
